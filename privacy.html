Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 June 2026

Short version: The Great Logout only asks for the data needed to send the email guide. The post generator runs in your browser. We do not use tracking ads, analytics cookies, or profiling on this website.

What data we collect

When you sign up for the email guide, we process the information you submit:

  • email address
  • optional first name
  • optional planned logout date
  • selected guide length
  • consent to receive the guide

For email delivery and unsubscribing, we also store technical records such as an unsubscribe token, signup time, unsubscribe time, scheduled email status, delivery message IDs, and delivery error messages where needed.

Why we use this data

We use this data to send the logout guide, follow-up check-ins, and related campaign emails you requested. The legal basis is your consent under Article 6(1)(a) GDPR.

We may also process limited technical data to operate, secure, and improve the website and email system. The legal basis is our legitimate interest under Article 6(1)(f) GDPR.

Email guide and unsubscribe

You can unsubscribe from the email guide at any time by using the unsubscribe link in any email. You can also contact us at support@thegreatlogout.org.

If you unsubscribe, we stop sending guide emails. We may keep a limited record of the unsubscribe status to avoid sending further emails and to document consent history.

Post generator

The post generator on the website runs in your browser. Text you type into the generator is used to create the downloadable image on your device.

Some email links can open the generator or request a downloadable SVG through the campaign API. In that case, the selected text, format, and color may be included in the URL request so the file can be generated.

Service providers

We use service providers to operate the campaign:

  • GitHub Pages for hosting the static website
  • Cloudflare for DNS, security, and the email signup API
  • Cloudflare D1 for storing email guide subscriber records
  • Postmark for sending transactional and guide emails
  • Ko-fi for voluntary contributions, if you choose to support the campaign there

When you leave this website through an external link, the privacy policy of the external service applies.

Cookies and analytics

At the moment, this website does not use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or tracking pixels. If this changes, this policy will be updated.

Server logs

Hosting, DNS, security, and email providers may process technical data such as IP address, request time, browser information, requested URL, and delivery logs to provide and protect their services. We do not use this data to profile visitors.

Retention

Email guide data is kept while your subscription is active. If you unsubscribe, we keep only what is needed to respect the unsubscribe request, maintain delivery records, and handle legal or operational questions. You can request deletion at any time.

Your rights

Under the GDPR, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data. You may also withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise your rights, contact support@thegreatlogout.org. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy when the campaign, website, or email system changes. The date at the top shows the latest version.
